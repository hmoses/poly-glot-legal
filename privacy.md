# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 22, 2026**

## Overview

Poly-Glot Prompt Studio ("the App") is developed by Harold Moses. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App.

## Data Collection

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The App operates entirely on-device.

**Specifically, we do NOT collect:**
- Personal identification information
- Usage data or analytics
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Prompt content you enter
- Any data whatsoever

## On-Device Processing

All prompt templates are stored locally in the app bundle. All text you enter into templates stays on your device. Nothing is sent to any server.

## In-App Purchases

Subscription purchases are processed entirely by Apple via StoreKit. We do not receive or store your payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs all purchase transactions.

## Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

## Changes

If we update this policy, the new version will be posted at this URL.
